Comprehensive Petroleum Services

We offer end-to-end solutions in the petroleum industry

Oil & Gas Brokerage

Zambis petroleum provides offshore and onshore brokerage, agency and integrated services to industry in relation to oil and gas activities in Africa. We organize strategic alliances in connection with oil and gas offshore and onshore activities. Our services aim to provide one-stop solutions to tighten the timeline, reduce risk and maximize cost effectiveness for oil and gas operators and their activities.

Onshore and Offshore Logistics

Zambis petroleum provides comprehensive integrated chain solutions to onshore and offshore production support services, offshore logistic support including onshore supply base, container hire, project management for offshore work and charter of marine vessels.

Retail and Distribution

Our extensive distribution network ensures reliable supply of petroleum products to businesses and industries across Zambia. We maintain strategic storage facilities and implement efficient distribution systems.

Equipment Procurement and Supply

Zambis petroleum optimizes the efficiency of your entire project chain. From regulatory license processing, sourcing of materials and manufacturing through delivery and return, we apply precision technologies and extensive experience to formulate logistics solutions specifically targeted to meet your challenges.
